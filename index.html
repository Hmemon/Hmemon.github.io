Personal Profile

My results for the online Myers Briggs test showed that I was of a mediator personality type. Mediator Personalities are guided by their principles rather than logic, mediators are led by the purity in their intent and not rewards or punishment. Mediators qualities allow them to communicate deeply with others and share their ideas in unique and creative ways. Mediators can be useful in team-based workplaces where communication is key. They can easily get their ideas across to others and make It easy to understand while also wanting to listen to other ideas.

An online learning styles test results showed that I was a combination of the 3 learning styles but leaning towards being a Visual learner.
